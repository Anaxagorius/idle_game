Executive Summary

Idle Empire Ultimate is a large-scale incremental game that begins as a simple clicker and evolves into a deep economic simulation spanning industries, technology, automation, space colonization, and multi-layer prestige systems.

Players start by manually producing coins and hiring workers. As they grow their economy, they unlock advanced production chains, research technologies, automation systems, planetary expansion, and eventually cosmic-level civilization management.

Vision Statement

Idle Empire Ultimate is envisioned as a modern, feature-rich incremental game capable of delivering hundreds of hours of progression. Beginning as a simple clicker and evolving into a civilization-scale economic simulator, it combines the accessibility of Cookie Clicker with the long-term strategic depth of Realm Grinder and Antimatter Dimensions, creating a compelling progression experience from the first click to galactic-scale automation and ascension.
